AI in Near Future

Technology is evolving faster than ever, many of which will soon be implemented to bring drastic changes to the modern society. In the recent years, one of the technologies had the focus of the majority of the world, and it is Artificial Intelligence (AI). There had been many advancements in AI in the past years, but it is still being improved, and the development can further change how some industries might work.

Artificial intelligence will actually change the way patients are treated. AI can perform many tasks in less time and at less cost, and it simplifies the lives of patients, doctors, and hospital administrators. Take the MRI, X-ray machines and CT scanners for example, their functions are undisputed. Robot doctors will gradually occupy the surgical site by making operations cleaner and more accurate. Compared with dermatologists, AI has been able to detect skin cancer more accurately, so through AI, disease detection will become more accurate.
